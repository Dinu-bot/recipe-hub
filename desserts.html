Dessert Recipes

Chocolate Brownies

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a baking pan.
  2. Melt butter and mix in sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
  3. Add cocoa, flour, salt, and baking powder. Mix well.
  4. Pour into pan and bake for 20-25 minutes.
  5. Let cool, cut into squares, and serve.

Classic Vanilla Pudding

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, whisk together sugar, cornstarch, and salt.
  2. Gradually add milk, whisking constantly.
  3. Cook over medium heat, stirring, until thickened (about 5-7 minutes).
  4. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla.
  5. Pour into bowls, chill, and serve.
